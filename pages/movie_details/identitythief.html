IDENTITY THIEF (2013)

SYNOPSIS

Identity Thief is a 2013 American road comedy film directed by Seth Gordon, screenplay by Craig Mazin, story by Mazin and Jerry Eeten and starring Jason Bateman and Melissa McCarthy. The film tells the fictional story of Sandy Patterson, whose identity is stolen by an unnamed woman, Diana.The film received negative reception from critics, but was a commercial success, grossing over $174 million worldwide against a budget of $35 million.
